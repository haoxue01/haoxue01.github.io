Hi! I am a Lecturer at the School of Computer Science and Engineering at University of New South Wales (UNSW Sydney), Australia. I obtained my PhD from The University of Western Australia in 2020. After completing my doctorate, I worked as a Research Fellow at the School of Computing Technologies at RMIT University and UNSW Sydney. I am an Associate Investigator at the UNSW node of the ARC Centre of Excellence for Automated Decision-Making and Society (ADM+S). My research is at the intersection of AI and smart city domains (such as intelligent transportation, energy, weather etc.), with a focus on spatiotemporal data modelling, time series forecasting, language generation based forecasting, and data-efficient time series representation learning. I am also interested in AI applications for wearable and IoT sensors.

I am open to research discussion and collaboration, please feel free to get in touch!
